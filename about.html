About Our Department

The Department of Computing and Informatics is dedicated to providing cutting-edge education and research opportunities in computer science, information technology, and related fields. Our mission is to prepare students for successful careers in the rapidly evolving technology landscape.

Our department is committed to fostering an inclusive environment where students from diverse backgrounds can thrive and contribute to technological advancements that benefit society.

Our Mission

To advance knowledge and educate students in computing and informatics through innovative teaching, research, and service to the community, preparing graduates who can contribute to the technological advancement of society.

Our Vision

To be a recognized leader in computing and informatics education and research, producing graduates who drive innovation and solve complex problems in the digital age.
