Admissions

Admission Requirements

Undergraduate Programs

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0
  • Strong background in Mathematics
  • SAT/ACT scores (optional)
  • Two letters of recommendation
  • Personal statement

Graduate Programs

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field
  • Minimum GPA of 3.2
  • GRE scores (preferred)
  • Three letters of recommendation
  • Statement of purpose
  • CV/Resume

How to Apply

  1. Create an account on our online portal
  2. Complete the application form
  3. Submit required documents (transcripts, test scores)
  4. Pay the application fee ($50)
  5. Schedule an interview (if required)
  6. Wait for admission decision (4-6 weeks)

Tuition & Fees

Undergraduate Programs

  • Tuition: $12,000 per semester
  • Technology Fee: $500 per semester
  • Lab Fee: $300 per semester
  • Total: $12,800 per semester

Graduate Programs

  • Tuition: $15,000 per semester
  • Technology Fee: $600 per semester
  • Research Fee: $400 per semester
  • Total: $16,000 per semester

Financial Aid & Scholarships

Merit Scholarships

Available for outstanding academic performance with awards up to $5,000 per year.

Need-Based Grants

Financial assistance for students with demonstrated financial need.

Work-Study Programs

Opportunities to work on campus while studying to help cover expenses.

Research Assistantships

Funding for graduate students working on faculty research projects.
